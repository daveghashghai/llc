Benefits of LLC

Limited Liability

A Limited Liability Company (LLC) is considered as a separate entity and personal assets of its owners cannot be touched or accessed to pay debts and other obligations of the LLC when involved in a law suit or legal collections.
This is considered to be one of the most important benefits.

Read More

Tax Benefits

A limited liability company is a “pass-through” tax entity. This means that loss, income, deductions, and other tax items of an LLC is carried over to its member or the members. The members report these items on their personal income tax and pay taxes at the individual tax rates and not as a company.

Read More

Ownership Flexibility

An LLC, through its internal operating agreement or articles of organization — can be controlled by its managers. Managers can be either members or non-members.
An LLC can have multiple owners who could run the business together.

Read More

Credibility

A business that is registered as an LLC shows potential customers, employees, vendors and other business related partners that you are serious about your business and your commitment to your business and customers.

Read More

Residency Requirement

There are no citizenship or residence requirements for ownership of a C Corporation or an LLC.

Read More

Build Business Credit

An LLC, can establish a credit profile that is distinct and separate from your personal credit profile.
